Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How many portable toilets do I need for my event?

As a general rule, plan for 1 toilet per 50-75 guests for a 4-hour event. For longer events or those serving alcohol, consider 1 toilet per 35-50 guests. We provide a free consultation to determine the exact number needed for your specific event.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance, especially for peak season (December-March) and weekends. For large events (500+ guests), book 2-3 months ahead. We do accommodate last-minute requests when availability allows.

What is included in the rental price?

Our standard rental includes delivery, setup, toilet paper, hand sanitizer, and basic cleaning. For VIP trailers, it includes running water, mirrors, lighting, and attendant service. Additional services like extra cleaning, attendants for standard units, and special placement may incur additional charges.

Do you provide attendants?

Yes! Attendants are included with all VIP trailer rentals and available as an add-on for standard toilet rentals. Our attendants are trained in hygiene maintenance, restocking supplies, and assisting guests. They ensure facilities remain clean and operational throughout your event.

What are your delivery and pickup times?

We typically deliver units 1-2 days before your event and pick them up 1-2 days after. Exact timing is coordinated based on your event schedule. Delivery is free within our standard service areas; locations outside these areas may incur a delivery fee.

Can the toilets be placed on uneven ground?

For optimal operation and safety, toilets should be placed on level ground. Our delivery team can assess your site and make recommendations. We provide leveling services for an additional fee if the ground requires preparation.

Do you offer long-term rentals?

Yes! We offer discounted rates for long-term rentals (weekly, monthly, or project-based). Our long-term service includes regular cleaning, waste removal, and maintenance. Contact us for custom quotes based on your project duration and needs.
